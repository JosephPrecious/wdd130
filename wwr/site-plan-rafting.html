Audience

Our water rafting website is designed to cater to adventure enthusiasts, thrill-seekers, and nature lovers who are seeking an exhilarating outdoor experience. Our primary target audience includes individuals aged 18 to 45, both solo adventurers and group participants, with an interest in exploring the great outdoors through water rafting activities. We aim to provide an engaging and informative online platform that appeals to both beginners and experienced rafters, fostering a sense of excitement and connection with nature. Scenario Questions: Scenario Question 1: Imagine a first-time rafter who is unsure about the difficulty levels of our rafting packages. How can they easily find information on the website to choose a suitable adventure? Answer: Our website features a dedicated section labeled "Rafting Packages" where users can find detailed descriptions of each package, including difficulty levels, required skills, and a brief overview of the experience. Additionally, we provide a comprehensive FAQ page addressing common concerns, and a live chat option is available for real-time assistance. Scenario Question 2: A group of friends is planning a rafting trip and wants to know if there are any special group discounts available. Where on the website can they find information about group rates? Answer: The "Special Offers" or "Group Discounts" section on our website provides detailed information about any ongoing promotions or discounted rates for group bookings. Users can also subscribe to our newsletter to receive updates on exclusive deals and seasonal discounts tailored for groups. Scenario Question 3: An environmentally conscious customer is interested in supporting eco-friendly businesses. How can they learn about our commitment to environmental sustainability on the website? Answer: Our "About Us" page highlights our commitment to environmental stewardship. Additionally, the "Sustainability Initiatives" section provides details on our efforts to minimize environmental impact, such as eco-friendly practices during rafting trips and partnerships with conservation organizations. Customers can find this information easily to align their values with our business practices.

Color Palette

Our website will feature a color palette inspired by the adventurous spirit of water rafting. The primary color, Denim Blue (#1E3A5F), symbolizes the flowing rivers and serves as the dominant hue. Terra Cotta (#9C6654) adds an earthy touch, creating a harmonious blend with Ash Gray (#B4B4B4), our neutral shade. To infuse excitement, Flame Orange (#FF6F61) acts as the vibrant accent color, adding pops of energy throughout the site. These colors will be implemented in the CSS with their respective names for a visually appealing and cohesive design. https://coolors.co/1e3a5f-9c6654-b4b4b4-ff6f61

Typography

Headings Montserrat sans-serif

Normal Text/Paragraph lato sans-serif

Our website will utilize Google Fonts to enhance the overall design and readability. Headings will be presented in Montserrat, a sans-serif font, to evoke a modern and bold aesthetic. For paragraphs, we have chosen Lato, another sans-serif font known for its clarity and versatility, ensuring a pleasant reading experience. The callout boxes will feature the Raleway font, contributing to a cohesive design while maintaining a contemporary and stylish look. These font choices aim to reinforce the adventurous and user-friendly atmosphere of our water rafting website.
